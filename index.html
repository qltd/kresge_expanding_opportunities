Expanding Opportunities

Working together to bring about a bold urban future in America's cities

Why Cities?

More than 80 percent of our nation's population lives in and around cities. We want to improve the quality of city life, particularly for the less fortunate

How is Kresge Expanding Opportunities in America's Cities?

Arts & Culture
We build strong, healthy cities by promoting the integration of arts and culture in community revitalization.
Detroit
We use a comprehensive framework to promote long-term economic opportunity in our hometown.
Education
We promote secondary access and success for low-income, first-generation and underrepresented students.
Health
We work to reduce health disparities among children and adults by addressing conditions that lead to poor health outcomes.
Human Services
We strengthen human services organizations that improve the quality of life and economic security of low-income and vulnerable people.
Environment
We help communities build environmental, economic and social resilience in the face of climate change.
